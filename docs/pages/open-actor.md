title:: The person side of Actor is not built yet

- The agent side of [[actor]] is now real: an agent is registered by its model and prompt, content-addressed so the same pair is the same agent.
- A person is still only vocabulary. In all the evidence a person acted a handful of times and left no name behind, and the acts a person really does — promoting, deprecating, discarding, merging — record no who at all today.
- So the open question is not *what is a person* but *what does recording a person's act require*: how we name a person, and whether attribution is settled the moment the first curation act needs a who.
- It waits for that first act. Until then Actor carries the kind but only ever holds agents.
