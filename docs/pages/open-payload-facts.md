title:: Which further facts should be lifted out of the Payload
type:: open-question

- A [[post]]'s language, whether it is a reply, whether it quotes another message, whether it carries pictures or video, and how much attention it got are all in the Payload already, and nothing reads any of them yet.
- Each earns a name of its own on the day something needs to ask about it in bulk, and not before.
