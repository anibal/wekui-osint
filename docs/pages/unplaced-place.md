title:: The Unplaced Place
type:: concept
status:: built

- Every [[event]] has exactly one **Unplaced** Place: the [[place]] where a [[post]] waits while we still do not know where it is about.
- The Event points at its own Unplaced Place directly. That pointer is what makes it the Unplaced Place — not its name, and not its Type.
  - Anyone can rename a Place or invent a Type, so a rule that mattered this much could not be left resting on a word.
- The Unplaced Place is never searched on and never counted as part of the Event's settled geography. It is a waiting room, not a location.
- It is built, and it is still waiting for something to hold: until Placement exists, a Post points at no Place at all — see [[open-placement]].
- **Unplaced** means *not yet worked out*; **No place** means *worked out, and the answer is nowhere*. One never stands in for the other — see [[no-place]].
