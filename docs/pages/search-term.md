title:: Search Term
type:: concept
status:: built

- A **Search Term** is one word or phrase that a [[search]] asks about, together with the **language** it is written in. A Search may carry several, or none at all.
- Terms narrow a Search from *everything said about these Places* to *what was said about these Places concerning these words*.
- A Search Term has no off-switch — see [[decision-2026-07-22-term-off-switch]].
