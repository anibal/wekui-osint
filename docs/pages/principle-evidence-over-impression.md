title:: Evidence over impression
type:: principle

- Claims about how well something works are answered with measurements, not opinions.
- Where it already operates, verbatim:
  - [[actor]] — people and agents are treated the same way on purpose: "it is the only way to ask, later, *how good is this agent at this job compared to a person*, and to answer it with evidence rather than impression."
  - [[query]] — recording exactly which names and terms each Query carried is what lets us "curate on evidence rather than opinion."
  - [[place]] — Proposed by exists so that reading Places out of Posts can be measured per Actor and improved "on evidence."
  - The whole [[decision-log]] — every entry cites measured evidence rather than taste.
